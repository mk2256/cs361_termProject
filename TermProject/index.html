Canvendar Assignment Helper

Overview

Cavendar allows you to quickly get assignment due dates from your Canvas page and allows you to quickly upload them to your Google Calendar instead of manually creating each event by themselves!! This saves you time and makes planning the beginning of the semester easy!

Quick Start

To begin using Canvendar, bring up the Assignments page on your desired class within Canvas.

Then, click the "Write to Calendar" Button and your assignments will be written on your G-Calendar.

Otherwise, select the various options under "Additional Actions".

Get Assignment Dates!

Take all due dates and make into calendar events! As easy as 1-step click!
